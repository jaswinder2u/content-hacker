  • How do you hack rank with Keyword Hacker?

    It’s simple- build a mission to out-rank your competition by matching or outpacing their content marketing efforts but do this for keywords with the highest projected ROI (we call Keyword Networth™)

  • Step 1 [ BUILD A MISSION ]

    Create a new mission by entering keywords you would like to rank naturally for along with geo targets, industry targets and your online conversion rate.

  • Step 2 [ VIEW MISSION REPORT ]

    Keyword Hacker's mission report shows you the organic web traffic and online sales for each keyword phrase. This gives you a projected monthly ROI (we call Keyword Networth™)

  • Step 3 [ HACK CONTENT STRATEGIES ]

    Hit the 'reveal' button to hack the content marketing strategies of these top competitors.

  • Step 4 [ VIEW CONTENT GOAL ]

    Keyword Hacker will automatically compare your content marketing strategy to the competition and set a "CONTENT GOAL"

  • Step 5 [ SELECT KEYWORDS ]

    Select phrases with high ROI! Keyword Hacker subtracts your content goal cost from the projected gross sales to give you the monthly keyword networth (ROI) you could expect if your site were to rank on page one of the search engines.

  • Step 6 [ FULLFILL CONTENT GOAL ]

    Now that you’ve selected keywords with the highest ROI and within your budget, its’ time to act and place your content orders. We will do the rest and send you the content once it's ready!
