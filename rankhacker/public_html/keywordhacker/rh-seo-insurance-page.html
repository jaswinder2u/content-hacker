THE RULES

You must notify us within 7 days of the time the page one slip occurs or the policy will be void. We will also send daily ranking reports but you are responsible for letting us know if a page 1 slip occurred.

We require FTP & Google Webmaster Tools access within 7 business days of the page 1 slip so we can perform a full technical audit and implement SEO best practices to mission critical areas.

We cannot guarantee that we will fix your SEO issues and push your keyword phrase back to page 1 of Google but we will guarantee that your keyword phrase will be placed on page 1 of Google via pay per click ads during the ‘activation term’.
